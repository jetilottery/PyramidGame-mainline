How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

[ top ]

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to automatically play the game to completion. Game play continues until all selections have been made.

[ top ]

How to Play

Select shields or press REVEAL ALL to reveal symbols.

Match any Winning Symbol to any Your Symbol to win the prize shown.

A Mystery Award may be triggered after all Your Symbols and Winning Symbols are revealed. When triggered, 3 to 5 fire arrows are lobbed at non-winning symbols.
Any symbols hit by arrows turn into Jade symbols with a 2x, 5x, 10x, or 20x multiplier. Revealed Jade symbol wins are multiplied by the multiplier value.

The total win amount is the sum of all winning prize values.

After all Your Symbols and Winning Symbol are revealed, or a Mystery Award is triggered, the game ends. A closing message appears, indicating the game’s outcome.

[ top ]

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game's outcome.

Wins are shown in currency.
